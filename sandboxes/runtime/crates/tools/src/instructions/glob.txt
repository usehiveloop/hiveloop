Fast file-pattern matching. Supports `**/*.js`, `src/**/*.ts`, etc. Results sorted by modification time.

- Skip glob if you can RipGrep instead (you usually want content, not paths).
- Truncated at 1000 matches. If `truncated` is true: narrow the pattern, switch to RipGrep, or spawn self_agent.
- Respects `.gitignore`.
- Multiple globs in one turn (parallel) is OK and often faster.
